The Rules

* Players' movements are one step forward (in the enemy direction) and diagonally.

* If any player can 'jump' over an enemy's player, it has to jump and remove the enemy's player. The jumping can be in any direction and also backward. If the player can jump again after jumping, it has to jump again.

* When any player reaches the end of the board he is becoming a king. the king can move in any direction (also backward) and can run more than one step.

* The winning comes when the enemy has no players or he has no movements to do.
